Creative Intelligence

Hook — Hold — Click — Convert

AI analyzes every creative at each stage of the funnel. Know exactly where you're winning, where you're bleeding, and what to do about it.

Hook
Are your first 3 seconds grabbing attention?
AI scores every creative's hook performance. See which angles capture attention and which waste budget with weak openings.
Hold
Are viewers staying past the hook?
Hold rate analysis reveals which creatives keep viewers engaged through seconds 3-15. Get specific recommendations for pattern interrupts and pacing.
Click
Which formats drive the most clicks?
CTR performance breakdown shows you which creative formats outperform — sometimes static images beat video. Know what to scale.
Convert
Is your creative aligned with your offer?
Conversion analysis reveals offer-creative misalignment. Find which combinations drive efficient conversions and which need A/B testing.
