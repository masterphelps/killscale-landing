Questions? Answers.

How is this different from Ads Manager?

Ads Manager is built for agencies with 100+ clients. KillScale is built for business owners who need clarity, not complexity. We show you what's working and let you act on it instantly.

Does it work with my existing campaigns?

Yes. Connect your Meta account and we'll pull in all your existing campaigns. You can manage everything from KillScale or keep using Ads Manager — your choice.

What's the first-party attribution pixel?

It's a tracking pixel you add to your site that captures conversions directly, bypassing Meta's restrictions. You get accurate revenue data even when Meta's reporting is delayed or incomplete.

What is dynamic lateral attribution?

Our proprietary multi-touch attribution system. It dynamically weights touchpoints based on their actual contribution to conversions — not just first or last touch.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. No contracts, no commitments. Cancel from your account settings and you won't be charged again. Your data stays accessible on the free tier.

Do I need to be technical to use this?

No. If you can upload an image and type a number, you can use KillScale. The pixel setup takes 5 minutes with our copy-paste instructions.
